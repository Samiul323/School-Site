About School

Every school is special in its own way and the same is with my school as well. It is a place where people from different backgrounds and temperaments come together with the purpose to learn. It offers magic in the form of experiments in laboratories, sports in the field, creativity in the art room and more. Schools are a place where we get to make friends, learn new things and grow every day. In fact, it is so hard to imagine a world without schools. They are the first place where we learn about life and many other things. Similarly, my school plays the same role in my life.
Schools are a place that not everyone has the luxuries of visiting and not everyone has a place for it in their hearts. A School is an institution that shapes the kid to make itself into a future that aligns with success. Success is never a one-time thing and years of efforts are behind it. A school consistently makes efforts to discipline and love children at the same time, showing them both the facets of life.
After all, When Life would examine students, it is never going to be according to defined subjects. A School makes sure that students gain every ounce of knowledge and fight back through every challenge with the best that they have Got.
